RATAN TATA

Ratan Tata is one of India's most respected business leaders and philanthropists. He served as the Chairman of the Tata Group, one of India's largest and most prestigious conglomerates, from 1990 to 2012, and again as interim chairman from 2016 to 2017. Under his leadership, Tata Group expanded significantly, both in terms of revenue and global presence.

AMBANI

Mukesh Ambani is one of the wealthiest and most influential business figures in India and the world. He is the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, telecommunications, retail, and various other sectors. Under his leadership, Reliance has evolved from a textile and petrochemical company into one of the most diversified business empires in India, with a significant global footprint.
